### [METR Developer Productivity Study](https://metr.org/blog/2025-07-10-early-2025-ai-experienced-os-dev-study/)
- **Source**: METR.org / Joel Becker et al.
- **Key findings**:
  - Experienced developers were 19% slower with AI tools (RCT)
  - 39 percentage point gap between self-assessment and reality
- **Relevance**: Core evidence for our "cognitive bias" principle

### [ETH Zurich AGENTS.md Study](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.21413)
- **Source**: arXiv / Thibaud Gloaguen et al.
- **Key findings**:
  - AI-generated context files decreased success by 3%
  - Human-written context files gave 4% marginal improvement
- **Relevance**: Validates our "human-written CLAUDE.md" recommendation
