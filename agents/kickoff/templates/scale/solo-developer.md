## Scale: Solo Developer

### Workflow Adjustments
- You are the only reviewer - AI should be extra thorough in self-review
- Commit frequently as your personal safety net
- Use git worktree for parallel AI instances when multitasking

### Communication
- Be concise - solo devs know their codebase well
- Focus explanations on "why" not "what"
- Proactively flag potential issues the developer might miss alone
